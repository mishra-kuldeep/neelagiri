Idly and Dosa batter

Dosa is a savoury pancake said to be originated in the Indian continent around 1000 years ago and guess what we, Indians still love it. Who can say no to those hot soft idlies and those super crispy dosa. Be it anytime anywhere dosa and idly is consider to be a classic meal. The joy that we get when we have our first bite into this amazing food is beyond words.

Idly is the steamed variety of the batter. Idly is the classic so are you! The taste of idly dipped in some hot sambar and chutney melts into our mouth. This nutritious food is a gift from India to the whole world.

Neelagiri brings to you the traditional home made idly dosa batter that has been passed down to generations, the way, it always used to be. So, why restaurants, when we can make it at home!

Ingredients: Rice,Black gram lentils,Fenugreek,Salt

Ada Dosa

Ada dosa is typically a dosa made with mixed variety of pulses, handpicked in proportions. Its considered to be a healthy alternative to the regular dosas. In today's world where everything we have is counted in terms of carbs and proteins, isn't it fascinating to know how our grandmas have come up with such amazing blends of rice pulses and spices to create some of the healthiest, tastiest foods ever been.

Have you ever wondered how someone would have come up with the idea of dosa. Well, it's too hard to guess. But it's not that hard to get a bite into those delicious dosas, now, with Neelagiri…just the way your mom makes it! Adding chopped onions, black pepper, cumin, asafoetida powder just before you make the dosa adds more flavour to the dosa. So enjoy!

Ingredients: Rice,Black gram lentils,Pigeon pea,Chickpeas,Green gram lentils,Fenugreek,Dried chilly,Salt

Appam

Appam is a south Indian crepe made with rice and coconut. Its crispy sides and soft center makes it an awesome pair with almost every curry we can think of.

A stew, made with vegetables and coconut milk is the best to pair with appams. Though not commonly seen in shops around here, appam holds a very special place in the hearts of people.

The trick to the best appam lies in the perfect batter and now that we have your backs for that who is up for a plate of appam and stew, grab our pack now!

Ingredients: Rice,coconut,Salt,May contain traces of pulses

Chutney podi

Chutney powder or podi is yet another spicy condiment from the south of India. It's a mix of coarsely ground pulses and spices. It's also called idly podi, gun powder in different parts of India. It's one of the easiest and tastiest dip for dosa and idly. Hot idlies dipped in ghee and covered in podi makes an amazing treat and a must try. There is no particular recipe for this podi as it differs from one kitchen to another. Its usually what moms think is the best.

We at Neelagiri, have done the same. Choosing a mix that will suit the taste of every customer, now you can enjoy a bit of tradition in every bite. Also cheers to the brain behind this awesome recipe!
