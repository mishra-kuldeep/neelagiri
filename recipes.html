Coconut chutney

Microwave the fresh frozen coconut for 2 to 3 mints.

Take 1 cup of coconut, add 1 spoon chilly powder, a small piece of ginger, and salt. Grind well for 1 mint with water. Heat oil, add mustards, dry chilly, curry leaves.

Pour the chutney and heat it for 1 mint in low flame.
