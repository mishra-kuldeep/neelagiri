About Us

It all started when we took a decision to move across the globe, a few years ago. Starting a new home, away from home was rather tough, and one thing that always connects us to our home land is food.

The search for authentic South Indian food made us ending up in starting our own kitchen. We believe the key for healthy food lies in simple ingredients and we try to keep our ingredients as simple as possible. Our recipes are not secrets nor are our ingredients. This authenticity makes our products unique and flavourful.

Neelagiri:

Just like food, we wanted our name, simple and unique that helps everyone feel about home. Neelagiri is a mountain range in southern India. Neelagiri translates to 'Blue Mountains'. This mountain range , belonging to the western ghats, turns strikingly blue due to a type of flower, called neela kurungi that grow on the surface.

This flower blooms just once in twelve years. This mountain range, is a tri junction for three major states of south India. The spices and tea from this mountains is savoured around the world. We didn't have to think much to choose this our as our brand name.
